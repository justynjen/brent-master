Of course not!

We want you to think big! By purchasing our Tenants-in-Common replacement properties, you can eliminate all of these concerns. Under TIC ownership arrangements, each property owner owns a fractional piece of a much larger property.

Our Acquisition Strategy Works

TIC properties that we offer for your replacement options should allow you to feel more secure about your ownership position. To that end, we only offer properties meeting these conditions.

  • Free-standing, single-tenant, triple-net-lease properties
  • Retail business locations with high credit quality tenants
  • Diverse Industries and prime locations all over the United States
  • Long-term leases (25-30years)
  • Size $500,000 to $10 million

Properties with triple-net-lease arrangements reduce your expenses and your effort by requiring tenants to pay for all taxes, utilities, insurance, and building repairs to each property. As you look at your current property choices, you'll see that the tenants you can expect are well-known, established nationwide chains in various states and multiple industries.
